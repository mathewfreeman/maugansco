The Hadley Huffman
Foundation

Learn More

This golf outing is proud to support the Hadley Huffman Foundation. Founded in 2019 while Hadley was a student at Wayne State University, the foundation is dedicated to addressing systemic barriers to medical access and supporting organizations that are working to advance equitable and accessible healthcare for underserved communities.

From a young age, Hadley demonstrated a deep commitment to equity, using her voice and platform to advocate for marginalized communities. Through attending marches, writing to lawmakers, and using her online presence to raise awareness, she recognized the importance of community engagement and the role we all play in challenging systemic inequities.

During her time at Wayne State University, Hadley participated in a medical volunteer program in Portugal that further shaped her perspective on global healthcare disparities in the United States. This experience, coupled with her passion for equity, led her to establish Students Against Medical Racism, an initiative set in the belief that accessible, equitable, and affordable healthcare is a fundamental human right, not a privilege.

Although she passed away in 2021, this foundation has become part of her legacy, ensuring that all people, regardless of race, class, or gender, can easily access safe, equitable, and affordable healthcare. Today, Hadley's vision lives on through the continued efforts of her family, friends, and the community she inspired.

Zeke Berry

#10 Defensive Back

Making an Impact
Off the Field

Zeke Berry is a defensive back for the Michigan Wolverines, known for his physicality, versatility, and instinctive playmaking in the secondary. A De La Salle High School standout, Berry has been part of Michigan's championship-level defense and continues to develop as a leader on and off the field.

Zeke Berry Foundation

The Zeke Berry Foundation is dedicated to supporting individuals and families experiencing homelessness and uplifting communities in need. Built on a commitment to giving back, the foundation focuses on providing resources, support, and meaningful outreach to those facing difficult circumstances.

From a young age, Zeke grew up in an environment where homelessness was highly visible. Seeing people struggle on a daily basis left a lasting impression and shaped his understanding of how real and widespread these challenges are. Those early experiences helped form a strong sense of responsibility to give back and be part of the solution.

This foundation is built on the belief that everyone deserves dignity, support, and the opportunity to move forward. Today, that vision continues to grow, with a commitment to expanding efforts, strengthening communities, and finding new ways to support those who need it most.
